## 三、个人信息资产梳理

```sql
-- 数据库个人信息字段识别
-- 手机: LIKE '%phone%' OR LIKE '%mobile%'
-- 邮箱: LIKE '%email%' OR LIKE '%mail%'
-- 身份证: LIKE '%id_card%' OR LIKE '%identity%'
-- 地址: LIKE '%address%' OR LIKE '%addr%'

-- 完整清单:
SELECT table_name, column_name 
FROM information_schema.columns 
WHERE column_name RLIKE 'phone|mobile|email|id_card|address|real_name'
  AND table_schema NOT IN ('mysql','sys','information_schema');
```
